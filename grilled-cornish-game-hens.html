Grilled Cornish Game Hens

Description

These grilled game hens were a wonderful diversion from our usual, more mundane (i.e. chicken) menu. I confess that I only had two hens for the 4 servings I was hoping for. Prior to marinading, I cut each of them in half lengthwise, so that each half had a wing and a drumstick. I placed the four halves in a glass baking dish and poured on the marinade. (I made 1/2 the marinade, since I had half the game hens!). My husband, who is the Grill Master, popped them on the grill and cooked them slowly as he would a large, bone-in chicken breast.

Ingredients

  • Grill
  • Cornish
  • Game
  • Hen

Steps

  1. Grill it!
  2. Corn it!
  3. Game it!
  4. Hen it!
